Elevators: A Brief History

Elevators Then and Now

Elevators have come a long way since the first conventional passenger elevator built in the 19th century. At the same time, however, the basic design of the lift has remained very similar to the original. Despite this, elevators still managed to change the way we live in modern society and continue to do so today. To learn more about the magnificent invention that is the elevator, we will go over its past, present, and future in a (very) brief history. Let's begin, shall we?
