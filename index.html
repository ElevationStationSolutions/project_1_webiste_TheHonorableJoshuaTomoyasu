Welcome to the Elevator Side of the Internet!

Elevators are something most of us use regularly, however we often overlook them and take them for granted. As such, elevators don't really get the appreciation they deserve. The purpose of this website is to gather useful information on elevators in one place and to enlighten the general public with important knowledge on one of the world's safest and most common forms of transportation. On this website, you will find pages on the history of elevators and tips on elevator safety. You will also find helpful links to other interesting websites regarding elevator education!

Why Study Elevators?

Elevators are pretty boring, right? You get inside, push a button, a cable pulls you up, and *presto!* You're at a different floor! But have you ever stopped to think about what is going on behind the scenes when you press that button? Do you think of how the elevator brings you upstairs so fast wihout you even noticing? If so, you're aready thinking like an elevator engineer! By taking some time and doing a little research, you will find that there is more behind elevators that meets the eye. Elevators are like cars; they are made by numerous companies, work in different ways, and change the way we live!
